About Me

I am a Mathematical Statistician in the Statistical Engineering Division at the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST). Prior to joining NIST I completed a combined B.S./M.S. in Appplied Math from The University of Colorado Boulder in May 2018. I will begin my PhD in statistics at Texas A&M University in the Fall of 2020.


Research Interests

Bayesian Statistics, Generative Modeling, Distributed Computing and Machine Learning.
